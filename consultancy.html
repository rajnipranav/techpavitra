Capabilities · 03

Expertise you can deploy — at programme scale.

For clients who need specialist engineering, integration and programme-management capacity — without carrying it in-house — TechPavitra provides consultancy and turnkey delivery tailored to government, PSU and large-enterprise needs.

Services

Six service lines — single engagements or combined into a turnkey programme.

Clients engage us for a single advisory brief, a multi-vendor integration, or a full-lifecycle turnkey programme — often starting with one and expanding into others as trust builds.

01

Technology consultancy & systems-engineering advisory

Independent technical advisory on architecture, technology choices, build-vs-buy decisions and programme structure — grounded in engineering, not slideware.

02

Multi-vendor integration

Integration of multi-vendor hardware and software into coherent operational systems, with interface management, test and acceptance responsibilities clearly owned.

03

Turnkey design, supply, install, O&M

End-to-end turnkey delivery — design, supply, installation, commissioning, operation and maintenance — under a single accountable contract.

04

Cybersecurity & digital-transformation advisory

Security assessments, architecture reviews and digital-transformation advisory for operators of regulated or critical systems.

05

Capability-building & knowledge transfer

Structured training, documentation, on-site handover and longer-term capability-building programmes — so your teams own the system after we exit.

06

Prime or sub-contractor on major programmes

Prime- or sub-contractor role on large-scale Central, State and multilateral programmes, with programme governance, reporting and compliance handled in-house.

How we deliver

A five-stage delivery discipline, applied to every engagement.

Every engagement — whether a two-week advisory sprint or a five-year turnkey programme — runs through the same five stages. That consistency is what makes engagement commitments survive contact with reality.

1

Scope & assessment

Understand the outcome, the environment, the constraints and the success criteria — written down, agreed and signed before we start.

2

Architect & design

Technology architecture, integration design, risk register and programme plan — produced as reviewable artefacts, not slideware.

3

Build & integrate

Manufacture, integration, configuration and test under controlled-quality procedures, with transparent progress reporting throughout.

4

Commission & handover

Site installation, operational acceptance, documentation pack and structured training so your teams are ready to own the system from day one.

5

Sustain & evolve

Operation & maintenance, spares and obsolescence management, and iterative capability upgrades so the system stays mission-ready.

Engagement models

Commercially structured for the kind of programme you’re running.

We structure engagements to match how you buy. Advisory work runs on time-boxed sprints. Integration and turnkey work runs on milestone-based fixed-price. Long programmes can run on framework contracts with scheduled work orders.

Model A Advisory sprint

Time-boxed advisory engagement — typically two to twelve weeks — producing a specific reviewable artefact such as an architecture assessment, a technology-options report or a programme plan.

Best for Early-stage decisions, go/no-go reviews, second-opinion mandates.
Model B Fixed-price milestone delivery

Defined scope, defined milestones, defined price — with clear acceptance criteria at each stage and structured change-control for anything outside the brief.

Best for Integration projects, turnkey deployments, capability-build work.
Model C Framework with work orders

Multi-year framework contract under which specific work orders are called down as needed — with pre-agreed rates, terms and governance.

Best for Long programmes, continuing advisory, panel appointments.
Model D Prime / sub-contractor on major programmes

TechPavitra as a prime contractor leading a full programme, or as a specialist sub-contractor inside a larger prime-led programme — with programme-office capacity and compliance handled in-house.

Best for Central / State tenders, multilateral programmes, large PSU contracts.
